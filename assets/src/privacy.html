Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information

Last updated: August 7, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how ByteBunny Selfbot ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Discord selfbot service and website.

By using ByteBunny Selfbot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Discord User ID: Your unique Discord identifier
  • Discord Username: Your Discord display name and tag
  • Server Information: Servers where you use ByteBunny
  • License Key: Your unique ByteBunny license identifier

Usage Information

  • Command Usage: Commands you execute through ByteBunny
  • Feature Usage: Which features and modules you use
  • Error Logs: Technical errors for debugging purposes
  • Performance Data: Usage statistics and performance metrics

Technical Information

  • Hardware ID (HWID): For license verification
  • IP Address: For security and access control
  • Device Information: Operating system and version
  • Application Logs: Technical logs for support purposes

3. How We Use Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

Service Provision

To provide and maintain ByteBunny Selfbot functionality

License Verification

To verify your license and prevent unauthorized use

Support & Maintenance

To provide technical support and improve our service

Bug Fixes

To identify and resolve technical issues

Analytics

To understand usage patterns and improve features

Communications

To send important updates and announcements

4. Information Sharing

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Security Purposes: To prevent fraud or abuse of our service
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our service

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using strong encryption protocols

Secure Storage

Information is stored on secure servers with restricted access

Access Control

Only authorized personnel have access to personal data

Monitoring

Systems are monitored for suspicious activity and breaches

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Active Users: While you have an active ByteBunny license
  • Inactive Users: Up to 12 months after license expiration
  • Support Data: Up to 24 months for troubleshooting purposes
  • Legal Requirements: As required by applicable law

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate information

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data

Portability

Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us through our Discord server or GitHub repository.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (if any)
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use advertising or social media trackers

9. Children's Privacy

ByteBunny Selfbot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We will post the updated policy on this page
  • We will update the "Last updated" date
  • We will notify users of significant changes through our Discord server
  • Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes
